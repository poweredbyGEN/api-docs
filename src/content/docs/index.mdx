  <section class="gen-splash__flow">
    <header class="gen-splash__flow-header">
      <span class="gen-splash__flow-label">The production pipeline</span>
      <h2 class="gen-splash__flow-title">
        Five steps. <em>Idea to rendered video.</em>
      </h2>
      <p class="gen-splash__flow-sub">
        Every endpoint on this API maps to one of these five steps. Follow them in order for your first video; branch freely once you know the shape.
      </p>
    </header>

    <ol class="gen-splash__steps">

      <li class="gen-step">
        <span class="gen-step__no" aria-hidden="true">01</span>
        <div class="gen-step__body">
          <p class="gen-step__tag">Step one</p>
          <h3 class="gen-step__title">Set up your <em>agent.</em></h3>
          <p class="gen-step__desc">
            One call to <code>/agents/:id/core</code> configures identity, personality, voice, inspiration, and the social accounts the agent will publish to. Every downstream call reads from this core — it's the single most important step for a new agent.
          </p>
          <figure class="gen-step__code">
            <div class="gen-step__code-lang">HTTP</div>
            <pre><code>PATCH /v1/agents/:id/core
X-API-Key: $GEN_API_KEY

&#123;
  "name": "Kai",
  "overview": "B2B SaaS founder audience",
  "personality": "...",
  "voice_id": "voice_01h...",
  "accounts": [&#123; "platform": "tiktok", "handle": "@kai" &#125;]
&#125;</code></pre>
          </figure>
          <a href="/reference/agent-core/" class="gen-step__link">
            Agent Core reference
            <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="14" height="14" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M5 12h14m-5-5l5 5-5 5" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" fill="none"/></svg>
          </a>
        </div>
      </li>

      <li class="gen-step">
        <span class="gen-step__no" aria-hidden="true">02</span>
        <div class="gen-step__body">
          <p class="gen-step__tag">Step two</p>
          <h3 class="gen-step__title">Generate <em>content ideas.</em></h3>
          <p class="gen-step__desc">
            The ideas engine pulls trending data across 10+ platforms, matches it to your agent's keywords and inspiration, and proposes videos with hooks, scripts, and selected assets. Refine with follow-ups; ground with deeper research when the stakes are high.
          </p>
          <figure class="gen-step__code">
            <div class="gen-step__code-lang">HTTP</div>
            <pre><code>POST /v1/agent/run
X-API-Key: $GEN_API_KEY

&#123;
  "agent_id": "agt_...",
  "intent": "generate_ideas",
  "with_research": true,
  "count": 5
&#125;</code></pre>
          </figure>
          <a href="/guides/content-ideas/" class="gen-step__link">
            Content Ideas guide
            <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="14" height="14" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M5 12h14m-5-5l5 5-5 5" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" fill="none"/></svg>
          </a>
        </div>
      </li>

      <li class="gen-step">
        <span class="gen-step__no" aria-hidden="true">03</span>
        <div class="gen-step__body">
          <p class="gen-step__tag">Step three</p>
          <h3 class="gen-step__title">Convert idea to <em>vidsheet.</em></h3>
          <p class="gen-step__desc">
            Clone a template, inject the idea's fields as variables, and you have a ready-to-edit vidsheet — rows of scenes, columns of content types, cells for each piece. Templates are the 80% path; building from scratch is the other 20%.
          </p>
          <figure class="gen-step__code">
            <div class="gen-step__code-lang">HTTP</div>
            <pre><code>POST /v1/vidsheet/from_idea
X-API-Key: $GEN_API_KEY

&#123;
  "agent_id": "agt_...",
  "idea_id": "idea_...",
  "template_slug": "talking-head-3-scene"
&#125;</code></pre>
          </figure>
          <a href="/reference/templates/" class="gen-step__link">
            Templates reference
            <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="14" height="14" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M5 12h14m-5-5l5 5-5 5" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" fill="none"/></svg>
          </a>
        </div>
      </li>

      <li class="gen-step">
        <span class="gen-step__no" aria-hidden="true">04</span>
        <div class="gen-step__body">
          <p class="gen-step__tag">Step four</p>
          <h3 class="gen-step__title">Edit and <em>generate.</em></h3>
          <p class="gen-step__desc">
            Tweak cells (prompts, scripts, variables), add or reorder layers (text, audio, clips, captions), then trigger generations per cell. Jobs are async — poll <code>/generations/:id</code> for status and output.
          </p>
          <figure class="gen-step__code">
            <div class="gen-step__code-lang">HTTP</div>
            <pre><code>POST /v1/vidsheet/:id/cells/:cell_id/generate
X-API-Key: $GEN_API_KEY

&#123;
  "generation_type": "video_from_text",
  "data": &#123;
    "prompt": "...",
    "model": "veo_3",
    "duration": 8
  &#125;
&#125;</code></pre>
          </figure>
          <a href="/reference/generations/" class="gen-step__link">
            Generations reference
            <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="14" height="14" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M5 12h14m-5-5l5 5-5 5" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" fill="none"/></svg>
          </a>
        </div>
      </li>

      <li class="gen-step">
        <span class="gen-step__no" aria-hidden="true">05</span>
        <div class="gen-step__body">
          <p class="gen-step__tag">Step five</p>
          <h3 class="gen-step__title">Render and <em>export.</em></h3>
          <p class="gen-step__desc">
            Composite all layers into a final video. Poll the same <code>/generations/:id</code> endpoint for render status. When it completes, you get a downloadable URL — or optionally publish straight to TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube.
          </p>
          <figure class="gen-step__code">
            <div class="gen-step__code-lang">HTTP</div>
            <pre><code>POST /v1/vidsheet/:id/cells/:cell_id/render
X-API-Key: $GEN_API_KEY

&rarr; poll GET /v1/generations/:generation_id
&rarr; status: "completed"
&rarr; output_resources: [&#123; file_url: "..." &#125;]</code></pre>
          </figure>
          <a href="/reference/rendering/" class="gen-step__link">
            Rendering reference
            <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" width="14" height="14" aria-hidden="true"><path d="M5 12h14m-5-5l5 5-5 5" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="2" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" fill="none"/></svg>
          </a>
        </div>
      </li>

    </ol>
